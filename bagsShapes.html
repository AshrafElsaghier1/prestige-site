SHIPPING & RETURNS +

Delivery is free for all orders over $70. Otherwise, delivery is $8

Once your product has shipped, it usually takes 2 to 3 business days in France, 3 to 7 in Europe. 5 to 15 for the rest of the world. If you have not received your products, feel free to contact us using our contact page.

You can return your product up to 14 days after receiving your order. Please make sure that the products are not worn, washed or damaged.

SHIPPING & RETURNS +

Every single product we made is hand made in a workshop located near Naples. This factory- specialized in luxury leather goods- was created in the early 1980's by Dino Levorino, a well known artisan in Italy for his know-how.

We are in daily contact with his son Gennaro to discuss the production and to talk about the development of new products.

More than a manufacturing workshop, they are genuine partners who believe in our project, our products and our vision of Leo and Violet since the first days of the brand.

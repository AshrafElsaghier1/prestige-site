FIRST PRODUCT

LE PETIT CARTABLE

Leo et Violette's story is ours. We are Léo Dominguez & Violette Polchi. Two Parisian lovers sharing our life for more than 8 years. Ever since we met, we always had the dream to develop a project together. By creating Le petit cartable in January 2013, we started to realize this dream. Our first Kickstarter campaign is launched!

OUR VISION

TRANSPARENCY

No intermediaries and no wholesalers. So no excessive margins. It's just between you and us. And this is what allows us to offer you a quality product at an affordable price. Welcome to the fashion of the 21st century.

NEW FACTORY

RELATIONSHIP

Our workshop is located next to Naples, Italy. It was founded in the early 1980s by Dino Levorino, a craftsman well known for his know-how. The workshop was taken over by his son Gennaro, with whom we talk every day, about the production and the design of new models. More than just a factory, they are real partners who have shared Leo et Violette's vision.

NEW IDEAS

FIRST POCHETTE

Our first leather pouch suitable for carrying your computer 13 inch and your accessories. Inner pocket with zip for storing your phone or your keys. Made in Italy.

OUR FLAGSHIP
